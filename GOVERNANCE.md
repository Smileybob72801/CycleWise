# RhythmWise Governance Model

_Last updated: October 2025_

---

## 🧭 Overview
RhythmWise is an open-source project created and maintained by **Daniel Simmons**.
Its governance model follows a **maintainer-led** structure: decisions are made
collaboratively with community input, but final authority and direction remain
with the maintainer.

This approach ensures that RythmWise continues to uphold its founding values of
privacy, transparency, and user autonomy while maintaining a coherent design
and roadmap.

---

## 👤 Maintainer Responsibilities
The maintainer is responsible for:

- Defining and communicating the project vision and priorities  
- Reviewing and merging pull requests  
- Managing releases and versioning  
- Ensuring code quality, security, and privacy standards  
- Approving contributors with elevated permissions (if any)  
- Acting as point of contact for community and security issues  

The maintainer may delegate specific areas (e.g., documentation, testing,
analytics) to trusted collaborators while retaining overall oversight.

---

## 🤝 Community Participation
RhythmWise welcomes community contributions through issues, pull requests, and
discussions.

Contributors are expected to:
- Follow the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)  
- Use the [DCO sign-off](DCO.md) on all commits  
- Respect review and merge decisions  
- Communicate constructively and professionally  

---

## 🧩 Decision-Making
- **Technical decisions**: Proposed through issues or PRs, discussed openly.
  The maintainer has final say after considering feedback.
- **Feature roadmap**: Defined by the maintainer, with community suggestions
  welcomed via GitHub Discussions or Issues.
- **Dispute resolution**: If disagreements arise, the maintainer will seek
  consensus; if none is reached, the maintainer’s decision stands.

---

## 🔐 Security & Privacy Decisions
Security and privacy are the highest priorities.  
Any changes affecting encryption, data storage, or network access require
explicit maintainer review and approval before merge.

---

## 🕰️ Future Evolution
As the project grows, additional maintainers may be nominated by Daniel Simmons
and confirmed through public discussion. A future **Core Team** model may be
adopted if multiple long-term contributors share sustained responsibility.
